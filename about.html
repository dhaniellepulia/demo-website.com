    Who we are?

    TEGYOUNG INT‘L TRADING, is a trusted importer and distributor of agricultural machineries. We take pride in distributing top-tier agricultural machineries and providing outstanding services to our customers to meet their needs and satisfaction. At TEGYOUNG, we are not just traders; we are partners in progress-where your success is our goal.

    Vision

    TEGYOUNG envisions to be the forefront provider of innovative agricultural machineries. We aim to elevate farming practices, enhance productivity, and cultivate a sustainable future for the Philippines through unparalleled agricultural technologies.

    Mission

    Fueled by the passion for agriculture advancement, TEGYOUNG mission is to supply state-of-the-art agricultural machineries, aiming to empower local farmers and contribute to the nation’s self-sufficiency in rice production. We strive to be the catalyst for positive change, bridging innovation and tradition for a thriving agricultural future.
